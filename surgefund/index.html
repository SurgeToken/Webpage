Surge Fund

About

Surge Fund is a charitable fund created for the victims of the August 16th attack on SurgeBNB. Once per day, victims of the attack can claim BNB from the fund, permitting there are funds available to claim.

What Caused The Exploit

SurgeBNB is the only Surge token that was vulnerable to this exploit. The exploit was from an error in the sell() function, which in the event of a sale would send BNB to a user before updating the total supply. A malicious smart contract could send it directly back to Surge, allowing them to purchase at a lower price because of the disparity in the total supply. This allowed the attacker to mint septillions of SBNB Tokens. Other Surge tokens do NOT trigger this contract functionality, and as such, no other Surge token is vulnerable to this exploit. We will continue to expand on the Surge Ecosystem, and donate a percentage of all future token taxes and profits toward the Surge Fund until the lost BNB has been recouped! Those who would like to donate to the fund are welcome to, as well!

Function 8. isVictim

This function verifies that your wallet address was in the listed addresses that were affected by the BNB Hack. Simply enter your address and click Query.

True = the address is entitled to reimbursement
False = Go to the discord server and contact a moderator.

Function 12. remainingBnbToClaimForVictim

This function shows you the amount of BNB you have claim to. As you claim from the contract over time, the value decreases.

A number will be returned that can be converted, click on the number and it will bring up the page shown on the last step of this tutorial.

Function 17. userCurrentClaim

This function shows you what is currently available to claim in the contract. As trades are made in the XSurge Ecosystem, the amount contributed to the contract will increase. So too will this value

Unit Converter

By selecting a blue linked value from the contract, it brings up the converter page shown to the right. Check the number next to BNB(1). That is your amount available to claim.

If you have any issues with the amounts of your wallet is not included in the victim list, contact any moderator on the Discord. We will point you in the right direction.

Function 3. Claim

Once connected to your wallet, Click the "Write" button. This will Claim your portion of the BNB that is currently in the pool.

Note:

The amount you can expect depends on how much BNB is in the pool, and how much BNB you have yet to claim.

i.e. If I have 10 BNB left to claim, and there is 100 BNB left in the pool to distribute to everyone, that means I get 10/100 or 1/10th of however much BNB is in the contract.

More Information

Users may claim once every 24 hours.
The minimum claim for each user is 0.00005 BNB.
To verify exactly what your claim is for that day, refer to Function 17 of the contract (usersCurrentClaim), outlined in the (Community Review) Section of this tutorial.
